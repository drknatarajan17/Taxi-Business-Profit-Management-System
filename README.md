# 🚖 Taxi Business Profit Management System

An AI-powered Taxi Business Profit Management System developed using **Python**, **Streamlit**, **Pandas**, and **Matplotlib**. The application helps taxi owners analyze revenue, expenses, fuel costs, driver payments, and overall profitability while providing intelligent business insights through an interactive dashboard.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Managing a taxi business involves tracking multiple financial aspects such as revenue, driver salary, fuel expenses, maintenance, insurance, taxes, EMI, and miscellaneous costs.

This application simplifies the entire process by providing a centralized dashboard that calculates business profitability and presents detailed analytics for informed decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Calculate daily taxi business profit
- Track owner income and operational expenses
- Analyze business performance
- Visualize revenue and expense trends
- Generate downloadable business reports
- Assist taxi owners in improving profitability
